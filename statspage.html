R Studio Simulation: Coin Toss Probability

Code in R Studio that returns probability results for throwing 100 coins with different probabilities of returning 1 or 0.

1. The output of the dbinom function. There is a 0.03006864 chance of flipping exactly 57 heads out of 100 tosses of 10 fair coins.

2. The output of the pbinom function. There's a 0.9333947 chance of flipping ‚â§57 heads (100 tosses of 10 fair coins).

3. The median (6) from 17 flips of 10 biased coins (head probability = 1/3).

4. Sum of heads in 4 trials of tossing 10 biased coins (head probability = 0.8).

5. Probability (0.06660531) of getting more than 57 heads out of 100 flips.

6. Chance of flipping more than 44 but less than 56 heads in 100 coin tosses.

7. Plots showing how changing the probability of heads affects coin toss outcomes.
